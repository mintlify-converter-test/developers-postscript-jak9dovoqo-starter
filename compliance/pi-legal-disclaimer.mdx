---
title: "Postscript Subscribers API"
description: "Send Identifier Data to create targeting segments"
---
The Postscript Subscribers API allows you to send data (“Identifier Data”) to Postscript to create targeting segments based on a match of the Identifier Data.

The Identifier Data cannot contain any sensitive or special categories of information as defined in applicable data protection and privacy laws, including the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA). Specifically, do not use the Postscript Subscribers API to share the following information with Postscript:

*   Social security number
*   Driver’s license number
*   State identification card number
*   Passport number
*   Security or access code, password, or other credentials that permit access to an account
*   Financial account number
*   Debit card or credit card number
*   Racial or ethnic origin, religious or philosophical beliefs, or union membership
*   Genetic data
*   Biometric information
*   Health information
*   Personal information concerning a consumer’s sex life or sexual orientation
*   Data derived from technology or a device that can locate a consumer within 1850 feet
*   Political opinions
*   Citizenship or immigration status
*   An alleged or actual commission of a crime
*   Information about individuals you have actual knowledge are under the age of 13
